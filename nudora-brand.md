## Brand Voice

### Feel
Confident · warm · grounded · science-backed · approachable · empowering.
Think: a trusted pharmacist friend who happens to be a great writer.

### Tone rules
- **DO:** Use plain, direct language. Short sentences. Active voice.
- **DO:** Lead with the real-life feeling ("wake up without the bloat") before the mechanism.
- **DO:** Back claims with the ingredient or strain name when space allows.
- **DO:** Be warm and human — this brand was born from a real family's frustration.
- **DON'T:** Use clinical jargon without an immediate plain-English follow-up.
- **DON'T:** Use diet-culture shame language ("lose that ugly fat", "fix your broken gut").
- **DON'T:** Overpromise with superlatives ("the world's best", "miracle cure").
- **DON'T:** Use passive, hedge-everything corporate copy ("may potentially support…").
- **DON'T:** List ingredients without connecting them to a real human benefit.

### Voice examples

| Instead of… | Write… |
|---|---|
| "May support healthy gut microbiome" | "Restores your gut balance — so digestion just works." |
| "Contains Akkermansia muciniphila probiotic strain" | "Akkermansia muciniphila — the next-gen probiotic that fires up your metabolism and seals your gut lining." |
| "Clinically studied formulation" | "Every strain is backed by research — not just on a label." |
| "Promotes weight management" | "Supports natural fat burning without starving yourself." |

## What to NEVER Say

- ❌ "Cure", "treat", "diagnose", or "prevent" any disease (regulatory)
- ❌ Weight-shaming language ("fat", "obesity", "ugly belly")
- ❌ "The only supplement you'll ever need" (overreach)
- ❌ Comparing negatively to named competitors by name
- ❌ Implying results without individual variation disclaimer

## FAQ Master Answers (use for any FAQ section)

**Who is Nudora for?**
Anyone who wants to support their gut health, metabolism, mood, or digestion — but especially women and moms who are tired of juggling handfuls of supplements. Men can take it too.

**How quickly will I feel a difference?**
Most people notice improved digestion and energy within 1–2 days. Ongoing benefits like mood balance, immune support, and metabolism changes build over weeks of consistent use.

**Is Nudora plant-based and allergen-free?**
Yes. 100% plant-based, vegan, dairy-free, gluten-free, soy-free. No sweeteners or synthetic additives.

**Do I need to refrigerate Nudora?**
No. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

**Is Nudora approved in Canada?**
Yes. Nudora holds Canadian NPN (Natural Product Number) certification.

**Can men take Nudora?**
Yes — the formulas support gut health, metabolism, and mood for everyone.
