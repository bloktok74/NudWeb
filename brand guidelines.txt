Nudora Brand Guidelines
1. Brand Essence

Brand Name: Nudora
Core Idea: A clean, simplified daily wellness system built around gut health, metabolism, sleep, mood, and GLP-1 support. Nudora’s current positioning emphasizes replacing “10–15 supplements” with a simpler 3-step wellness system for gut, metabolism, and mood/sleep support.

Brand Promise:
Feel lighter, more balanced, and more supported from the inside out.

Brand Belief:
Your body does not work in isolated parts. Gut, energy, mood, sleep, digestion, metabolism, and recovery are connected.

Simple Brand Line:
Support your whole body from within.

2. Positioning Statement

Nudora is a premium wellness supplement system for people who want a simpler way to support digestion, metabolism, energy, sleep, and mood, especially those navigating GLP-1-related lifestyle changes or gut-related discomfort. The brand currently frames its system around Green for gut support, Orange for metabolism support, and Purple for sleep and mood support.

3. Brand Personality

Nudora should feel:

Premium but approachable
Clean, elevated, trustworthy, and not overly clinical.

Science-backed but human
Use pharmacist-formulated, clinically studied, clean ingredient, and gut-brain-metabolism language carefully, but avoid sounding cold or complicated.

Calm but effective
The visual tone should feel like spa wellness meets modern supplement science.

Supportive, not shame-based
Avoid aggressive weight-loss guilt language. Focus on relief, rhythm, balance, energy, confidence, and feeling better.

4. Brand Voice
Voice Attributes

Clear: Explain benefits in plain English.
Warm: Talk like a trusted wellness guide.
Confident: Make the product feel intentional and premium.
Balanced: Avoid miracle-cure language.
Modern: Short sentences, clean hooks, crisp benefit statements.

Example Voice

Instead of:
“Lose stubborn fat fast and destroy bloating.”

Use:
“Support your gut, metabolism, and daily rhythm — in one simple system.”

Instead of:
“GLP-1 is ruining your body.”

Use:
“GLP-1 journeys can change more than appetite. Nudora helps support the systems that keep you feeling balanced.”

5. Messaging Pillars
Pillar 1: Gut First

Gut health is the foundation of how people feel day to day. Nudora’s site directly connects gut health with energy, skin, mood, digestion, and hormones.

Key phrases:

Trust your gut.
Start with your gut.
Gentle daily digestive harmony.
Support your natural gut rhythm.
Less bloat. More balance.
Pillar 2: The 3-Step System

Nudora should be presented as a system, not just individual capsules. The brand currently positions itself as a 3-step daily system for gut, metabolism, and sleep/mood support.

Key phrases:

Gut. Metabolism. Sleep.
Three steps. One daily rhythm.
Simple wellness, color-coded.
Replace supplement overwhelm with a daily system.
Pillar 3: GLP-1 Support

Nudora can speak to people on GLP-1s or interested in GLP-1 pathway support, but claims should remain careful. The site describes the system as GLP-1 support and metabolism pathway support.

Key phrases:

Built with GLP-1 support in mind.
Support the systems your body depends on.
For your gut, energy, sleep, and recovery.
Whether you’re on GLP-1 or not.
Pillar 4: Clean, Thoughtful Formula

Nudora emphasizes plant-based formulation, no additives or sweeteners, and being free from dairy, gluten, and soy.

Key phrases:

Clean daily support.
No unnecessary extras.
Plant-based wellness.
Designed for real life.
6. Product Architecture
Nudora Green

Role: Gut support / digestive harmony
Color Mood: Sage, aloe, botanical, clean, fresh
Benefit Language: Constipation, bloating, digestive slowdown, gut rhythm, comfort, balance. Nudora Green is described as a pharmacist-formulated blend of botanicals, minerals, and probiotics designed to support natural gut rhythm.

Creative Direction:
Spa green, aloe, green tea, soft botanical shadows, marble/limestone, clean center space, fresh morning light.

Sample tagline:
Feel lighter from the inside out.

Nudora Orange 2.0

Role: Metabolism, energy, weight-management support
Color Mood: Warm orange, citrus, ginger, sunlight, active energy
Benefit Language: Metabolism, energy, endurance, appetite support, daily function. Nudora Orange 2.0 is positioned around metabolism, natural fat-burning support, energy, and GLP-1 activation support.

Creative Direction:
Warm citrus gradients, orange slices, ginger, golden sunlight, clean modern fitness/wellness energy.

Sample tagline:
Fuel your daily rhythm.

Nudora Purple

Role: Sleep, mood, calm, recovery
Color Mood: Lavender, plum, soft cream, nighttime calm
Benefit Language: Stress, mood, sleep, anxiety support, calm, clarity, recovery. Nudora Purple is positioned for stress, mood, sleep, and anxiety support with gut + brain double action language.

Creative Direction:
Lavender botanicals, soft moonlight, silk fabric, calm bedroom/spa cues, slow-motion visual language.

Sample tagline:
Rest deeper. Reset better.

Overall Look

Nudora should look like:

Premium wellness + modern clinical trust + soft spa lifestyle.

Avoid loud supplement-bro graphics. Avoid cheap Amazon-style before/after layouts. The visual identity should feel closer to luxury skincare, clean wellness, and boutique apothecary.

Visual Keywords
Soft marble
Limestone texture
Warm white
Botanical shadows
Blurred ingredient foregrounds
Clean negative space
Premium bottle hero shot
Soft natural sunlight

Calm editorial composition

Warm White
Use for clean backgrounds, ad space, website blocks, product staging.

Cream / Limestone
Use for premium softness and warmth.

Soft Charcoal
Use for body text and elegant contrast.

Muted Gold / Champagne
Use sparingly for premium accents.

Product Colors

Nudora Orange: Citrus orange, ginger, warm amber

9. Typography Direction

Use typography that feels clean, modern, and premium.

Recommended Style

Headlines: Elegant sans-serif or refined serif
Examples of style direction: modern wellness, luxury skincare, editorial health.

Body Copy: Clean sans-serif
Readable, calm, not too techy.

Ad Text: Bold, high-contrast, minimal
Use short lines with strong hierarchy.

Typography Rules
Keep headlines short.
Avoid cluttering product images with too much copy.
Use generous spacing.
Let the bottle and background breathe.
Use uppercase sparingly for emphasis.

14. Compliance-Safe Language

Use “supports,” “helps support,” “designed to support,” “promotes,” and “encourages.”

Avoid medical-cure language:

Cures constipation
Treats anxiety
Fixes insomnia
Reverses metabolic disease
Guaranteed weight loss
Doctor-proven results unless substantiated very specifically

Better wording:

Helps support digestive regularity.
Supports calm, mood, and restful sleep.
Designed to support metabolism and daily energy.
Helps reduce occasional bloating.
Supports your body’s natural rhythm.

16. Brand Do / Don’t
Do
Lead with the system.
Keep visuals clean and premium.
Use benefit-led but compliant wording.
Show the three colors clearly.
Make each product feel distinct but connected.
Use calm confidence.
Don’t
Make it look like a cheap weight-loss supplement.
Overuse medical claims.
Use fear-based body language.
Overcrowd product imagery.
Mix random colors outside the Green / Orange / Purple system.
Make the brand feel too clinical or too spiritual.
17. One-Sentence Brand Summary

Nudora is a clean, color-coded wellness system designed to support gut health, metabolism, sleep, mood, and whole-body balance — without supplement overload.
